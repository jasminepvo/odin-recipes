Fried Rice

Description

Fried rice is a dish of cooked rice that has been stir-fried in a wok or a frying pan and is usually mixed with other ingredients such as eggs, vegetables, seafood, or meat. It is often eaten by itself or as an accompaniment to another dish.

Ingredients (Serving Size: 6)

  • 3 cups of day old rice
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 3 stems of green onion, diced
  • 1/2 bag of frozen mixed vegatables
  • 5 eggs
  • Optional protein: chinese sausage, shrimp, chicken
  • Seasoning: Black Pepper, Fish Sauce, Oyster Sauce, Mushroom Bouilon, Soy Sauce

Directions:

  1. Heat oil in wide saucepan, add garlic and green onion once pan is hot. Sautee on low heat.
  2. Add thawed mixed vegatables and mix till warm.
  3. Add rice and mix.
  4. Add desired amount of seasoning.
  5. Push rice to one side of pan. Add a little bit of oil and crack eggs. Constantly stir eggs till you reach a scrambled consistency.
  6. Optional: Add cooked protein.
  7. Mix everything together and serve.
